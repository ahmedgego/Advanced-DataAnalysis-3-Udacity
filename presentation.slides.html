Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to look at the relationships between duration and usertype , age of member , gender of member and user type.

Dataset Overview¶

The dataset contains 325107 entries with 15 features including(tripduration,starttime,stoptime,start station id,start station name,start station latitude,start station longitude,end station id,end station name,end station latitude,end station longitude,bikeid,usertype,birth year,gender)

Distribution of months¶

The number of trips are increased in (may to oct) this months of summer in the first months of the year the trips are decreased because to winter
